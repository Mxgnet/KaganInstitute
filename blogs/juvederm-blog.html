About Juvederm

Though you may have considered getting Juvederm before now, you may not have looked into it very deeply. Before you book your appointment to receive this filler, you’d probably like to know what exactly it is. Here’s an in-depth look at what this filler does, and how it can help you look younger and healthier.

All About Your Skin

Before we talk about what this filler can do for you, you need to understand the way your skin works. Your skin is made up of many different chemicals, including proteins called elastin and collagen. These proteins serve several purposes, including providing your skin with its structure and allowing it to stay elastic. This elasticity is what makes it smooth back out after you’ve made facial expressions and relaxed the muscles.

However, as you get older, the chemical makeup of your skin begins to change. The amounts of collagen and elastin begin to reduce, and the creases that form when you make facial expressions start to become permanent wrinkles and folds. Gravity also begins to take a firmer hold, which causes sagging to happen.

What Is Juvederm?

This is a hyaluronic acid-based filler. Hyaluronic acid is a sugar that is naturally found in various parts of the body. This includes your skin, your connective tissues, and your eyes.

This filler is an injectable, which means that a very fine and precise needle is used to apply the product to different areas. It is also applied at different depths underneath your skin to help you achieve the results you want. There are several different types of this filler, and different ones will be applied to different parts of the face to help you achieve the best results.

Voluma XC

This particular type is designed specifically to provide volume to the cheek area. As your skin begins to lose collagen and elastin, your cheeks are one of the first areas to be affected. You’ll lose a significant amount of volume which makes you look older. The skin will also sag more.

Voluma XC will help put back the volume you may have lost. This makes your face look fuller, younger and smooths out sagging and wrinkles in the area.

XC and Vollure XC

Because facial movements will cause and exacerbate wrinkles, the area around your nose and mouth tends to be very prone to getting wrinkled. This is because the areas move around so much.

Combine this with a loss of volume in the area, and it’s easy to see why the skin gets so wrinkled. XC and Vollure XC help to fill in smile lines, or wrinkles that have appeared around the nose and mouth.

Volbella XC and Ultra XC

Some people have naturally thin lips, while others tend to lose volume in the lip area as they age. Regardless of the reason, thin lips can make you look unfriendly and serious.

If you’re looking for a non-surgical solution to your thin lips, then Volbella and Ultra XC can help. These two fillers will offer you plump, full lips for a fresh and young look.

How Does It Work?

When it’s injected into your skin, the hyaluronic acid inside this filler will accomplish several goals. As soon as it’s injected, the product will provide fullness to the areas where it’s applied. The hyaluronic acid in it will then begin to work, stimulating your body’s collagen production. Over time, the hyaluronic acid is safely absorbed by your body, leaving just the collagen behind.

Hyaluronic acid also serves another purpose. One of the things it’s known for is its ability to retain moisture. Keeping skin hydrated is an important part of keeping it young and healthy-looking. By attracting moisture, you’ll find that you’re able to enjoy your results from these injections for a longer time.

The Benefits Natural-Looking Results

When it comes to getting work done, many people are concerned about how natural their results will look. Fielding questions from friends and family about what you’ve had done can make you feel awkward and uncomfortable.

But when you get this filler, you’ll find natural-looking results. Although you’ll notice a change to your appearance immediately after treatment is done, they will be subtle, helping you to avoid those uncomfortable questions.

Safe Ingredients

Hyaluronic acid is the primary ingredient in this filler. Many fillers contain synthetic ingredients or ingredients that you may not feel comfortable injecting into the skin especially if you’re particularly sensitive.

But hyaluronic acid is a sugar that’s naturally found in your body. When you receive this filler, all it’s doing is injecting a chemical that’s already present in your body, and stimulating the production of a protein that your skin is made up of naturally.
