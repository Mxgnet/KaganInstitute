Lip Enhancement

As we age, our bodies start to produce less collagen, an essential protein that keeps our skin plump and youthful. This can impact many areas of the body, and it is not uncommon to be dissatisfied with naturally thin lips. At Kagan Institute, we want to help each of our clients maintain a youthful aesthetic. What Is Lip Enhancement?

Lip enhancement refers to any treatment that is intended to enhance, improve, or balance the shape or size of lips. There are a variety of methods available to achieve enhanced lips. While lip implants are a popular treatment option for enhancing the appearance of lips, not everyone is interested in pursuing a surgical procedure. Our professionals offer laser and injectable treatment options to improve the appearance of lips.

Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are a type of anti-aging injectable featuring a synthetic form of hyaluronic acid, also known as HA. Juvederm is one of the most popular brands of hyaluronic acid-based fillers. There are several formulations of this FDA-approved injectable, most ending with the letters “XC”. XC refers to the presence of lidocaine, an injectable anesthetic to ensure your comfort during and after treatment.

Juvederm can be used to improve the contours of the mid-face, smooth fine lines and fill in moderate-to-severe facial wrinkles and folds. However, there are a couple of formulations specifically designed to enhance the lips and eliminate smile lines. These are Juvederm Ultra XC and Juvederm Volbella XC.

Laser Lip Enhancement

Laser lip enhancement is a non-surgical, non-invasive treatment option. With the assistance of laser technology, this treatment encourages collagen production. Collagen effectively supports and firms tissues. Not only may this help plump lips, it can also help refresh lips impacted by signs of aging.
