Restylane

Kagan Institute offers Restylane dermal fillers. These are gel fillers that are considered hydrophilic, or “water-loving.” This unique filler spreads through the dermal tissue to effectively bind to water molecules, achieving a volumized appearance. They help to augment lips, smooth lines around the mouth, nose, and chin, and reduce the appearance of scars and under-eye hollows.

They all use a synthetic form of hyaluronic acid (HA) as their active ingredient. HA is found naturally in the body, and helps to stimulate and strengthen the body’s atural production of collagen. It is also critical for supporting and moisturizing the skin.

Contact Kagan Institute today to schedule a consultation at our California offices in Encino or Los Angeles and discover the benefits of the Restylane® family of fillers for non-surgical skin smoothing and lip enhancement.

How Does Restylane Work?

Fillers are injected at the site of the targeted area, and your maximum safety and comfort is the priority throughout the process. The fillers act as a sort of support system underneath the skin, with the hyaluronic acid binding to collagen to bolster the skin’s volume. We use these fillers to lift patients’ appearance and restore volume, but with the assurance that you will still look natural and like yourself.

The fillers are not permanent. They are eventually naturally absorbed and processed by the body. However, to help achieve and maintain the best results, a series of regular treatments may help you preserve the rejuvenated results.

Benefits of the Dermal Filler

  • Achieves fuller but natural results when enhancing lips
  • Plumps the folds, lips, and wrinkles to create volume for a healthy, youthful appearance
  • Safe procedure that achieves results that may last for up to six months to a year

How Long Will My Results Last?

This dermal filler’s effects are temporary, but long-lasting. Depending on the site of injection and type of treatment, fillers may last for up to a year. Depending on your customized treatment plan, our professionals will discuss how many treatment sessions you may need and what you can expect during the treatment process. Although Restylane fillers create results you can immediately enjoy, this dermal filler can also be a great way to manage visible signs of aging with a long-term anti-aging plan.

Are There Any Side Effects from Treatment?

You may experience some bruising, redness, or swelling at injection sites following treatment. However, this typically subsides within 24 hours of treatment.

Potential Candidates

  • Frustrated with sunken cheeks or under eyes
  • Generally healthy adults with realistic expectations and goals
  • Seek a non-surgical treatment for folds, lines, and wrinkles
  • Wish to rejuvenate thin lips to make them fuller
