Radiesse in Los Angeles and Beverly Hills

We all want to have young-looking skin for as long as possible. However, age robs us of the collagen that provides the volume, flexibility and strength we need to maintain our dermal health. Outside elements are also a factor in the condition of our skin, as the sun, harsh weather and other aggressors take their toll. With Radiesse at the Kagan Institute in Los Angeles, you can enjoy a solution to these age-old concerns.

Radiesse® is a next generation injectable dermal filler that replenishes lost collagen. This allows our patients to attain the natural-looking, healthy skin they desire. Our practitioners have seen excellent results with this product and is thrilled to offer it as part of our comprehensive selection of anti-aging treatments. Our staff looks forward to working with various Radiesse patients in the greater Los Angeles area to achieve their aesthetics.

Is Radiesse Right for Me?

Most generally healthy adults may be able to benefit from Radiesse. Typically, they are looking to correct frown lines or deep wrinkles around the mouth or cheeks. If you are bothered by creases and grooves on your face, but are not interested in more invasive surgery, then this filler may be a good option for you. This dermal filler is longer lasting that hyaluronic acid-based fillers and also contains lidocaine for increased comfort during the treatment.

Benefits

  • Safely restores a natural, youthful, lasting look
  • Stimulates the body to produce new collagen
  • Results are immediate and long-lasting between 8 to 16 months
  • Treatment is quick — takes about 10 to 15 minutes

How Does It Work?

Radiesse treatments typically take less than 30 minutes and are performed in our Los Angeles area offices. A small needle injects the gel into the targeted tissue, and that is it! There is minimal discomfort or downtime associated with this injectable.

The key to Radiesse is its ability to stimulate the production of new collagen. When the gel is injected, it fortifies the structure of your skin and increases its volume. This results in natural-looking wrinkle correction, which reduces the signs of aging.

What Is the Recovery Process?

It is normal to experience bruising, swelling, or tenderness immediately following your Radiesse treatment session. These side effects should subside within a few days of treatment.

Radiesse typically provides results that last for about one to two years after the gel has been absorbed by the body. This is because the new collagen that was produced by the gel of the filler will now take its place. However, results will vary for each person, concern, and treatment area.
