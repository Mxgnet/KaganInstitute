What Is Jeuveau?

Jeuveau™ is the latest injectable created by Evolus to be approved by the FDA for the temporary treatment of moderate-to-severe aging signs within skin. It is being recognized as the latest and greatest BOTOX alternative. Similar to BOTOX, Jeuveau is an acetylcholine release inhibitor and neurotoxin, meaning it prevents wrinkle-causing muscle contractions to preserve smooth and younger-looking skin. What makes Jeuveau different from other neurotoxins is that it is the first 900 kDa molecule toxin since BOTOX was released.

Alternative neurotoxin treatments such as Dysport and Xeomin are composed of smaller kDa molecules. Smaller molecules do not provide the same longevity as Jeuveau’s 900 kDa formulations. Jeuveau is also manufactured with Evolus’s Hi-Pure technology. This enables the product longevity compared to other neurotoxin products for longer-lasting results.

How Does It Work?

During an initial consultation, a personalized plan is created that is tailored to your unique skin needs and aesthetic desires. Jeuveau is delivered beneath the surface of skin through a series of small injections. Once delivered, the unique injectable substance works by binding to the underlying wrinkle-causing muscle. Once bound, the surface of skin is smoothed and the nerve receptors that allow the muscle to contract are blocked. This enables the temporary prevention of wrinkles from forming. The treatment process is quick and causes minimal discomfort.

You can notice visible improvements in the youthfulness of your skin immediately following treatment. Results from treatment can last for a few months and continued treatments are suggested in order to maintain results. With this revolutionary injectable, you can achieve younger-looking skin in no time!

Is It Right for Me?

Jeuveau can only be used for dynamic lines and wrinkles, meaning it can only be used to treat signs of aging caused by natural facial movements. This means it cannot be used to treat signs of aging caused by factors like sun damage, smoking, or similar environmental effects. However, most generally healthy adults seeking non-surgical methods of smoothing their skin may qualify as potential candidates to benefit from this injectable.
