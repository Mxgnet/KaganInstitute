What Is BOTOX?

BOTOX® is an FDA-approved treatment designed to target the main cause of fine lines and wrinkles in the skin. As we make facial expressions, such as smiling or laughing, specific muscles contract. These contractions create grooves in the skin, that become more prevalent over time. BOTOX in Los Angeles targets these muscles and restricts their movement to prevent the creation of deep creases in the skin. The results of this treatment can last from 2-6 months, but the average results last for approximately 3-4 months. Results will vary based on each person and their individual treatment plan.

When you come in for your consultation, our professionals will examine your skin and listen to your goals for the treatment. The treatment itself is a series ofinjections to the targeted area. The BOTOX injections use a special formula designedto help relax the muscles in the treatment area and help reduce the signs of agingon the surface of your skin. You will notice the results improving in the weeksfollowing your treatment the muscles remain relaxed.
