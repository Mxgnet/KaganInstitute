Belotero

Are you unhappy with fine lines, wrinkles, and volume loss within your skin? Has the natural aging process caused your complexion to appear dull or aged? The development of signs of aging is inevitable. Thankfully, there are non-invasive and effective treatments available for maintaining the youthful appearance of your skin. The professionals at Kagan Institute located in Encino and Los Angeles, CA, offer personalized Belotero treatments for reclaiming a smooth and younger-looking complexion!

What is Belotero?

Belotero® is a minimally invasive and safe injectable designed to target and reverse the negative signs of aging on the surface of the skin from within. It is specially formulated with a synthetic form of naturally-occurring substance in the body known as hyaluronic acid. Hyaluronic acid is responsible for skin hydration and elasticity. Once delivered, Belotero works to restore diminished hyaluronic acid and instantly plump-up the surface of skin for reclaimed youthfulness. With this revolutionary injectable, you can smooth away fine lines and wrinkles instantly, and achieve the rejuvenated and younger-looking complexion you desire.

What to Expect?

Like with any aesthetic treatment, an initial consultation is first performed in order to develop a treatment plan that is tailored to your skin needs and aesthetic goals. During treatment, Belotero is gentenly delivered beneath the skin in the targeted treatment areas through a series of tiny injections. Once delivered, Belotero instantly replenishes the underlying layers of skin tissue, and restores youthful volume. This is effective at diminishing lines and wrinkles, as well as enhancing youthful facial contours.

The treatment process is quick, and there is generally no downtime associated with the process. You should be able to resume most of your everyday activities. There may be some minor bruising or swelling in the treatment area, but this should subside shortly after the treatment.

Results from treatments may last for a few months. However, multiple treatments a few months apart are suggested in order to maintain rejuvenated and younger-looking skin. With this revolutionary filler, you can achieve skin-transforming results without the need for surgical measures.

What Can Belotero Improve?

Treatments are safe for all skin types and can be used to improve the appearance of fine lines in common problem areas including:

  • Crow’s feet
  • Nasolabial folds
  • Marionette lines
  • Frown lines
  • Between the brow lines
  • And more!
