What Is Juvederm?

Juvederm is a line of non-invasive dermal fillers that are designed to diminish aging flaws within skin. Dermal fillers are composed of a specialized substance known as hyaluronic acid. Hyaluronic acid is a naturally occurring substance in the body that is responsible for the hydration and volume of skin. Once delivered, this specialized formulation works by binding to the water particles in skin causing the tissue to plump-up. This is effective at restoring lost volume while instantly smoothing the surface of skin. Each product on the Juvederm line of fillers is specially formulated to address specific common areas within skin. This enables treatments to benefit a range of common problem regions within your complexion with results that you will love.

What to Expect

Like with any aesthetic treatment, an initial consultation with our professionals in the Los Angeles area is first required to create a plan for treatment that is tailored to you and your skin needs. Juvederm is administered through a series of small injections in the targeted areas of skin. The injection process causes minimal discomfort. Once delivered, the injectable works to instantly plump-up the surface of skin and restore lost volume.

The process is quick, and since it is non-invasive, there is no required downtime following treatments. You can notice immediate improvements in skin with results for a smoother complexion and more youthful facial contour. Continued treatments are suggested in order to maintain results.

Potential Candidates

Most generally healthy adults may qualify as candidates for Juvederm. This injectable treatment may be administered for most types of skin. Dermal fillers may be an appealing option for individuals frustrated with the signs of aging but hesitant to pursue a surgical procedure.
